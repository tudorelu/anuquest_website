ANUQuest

ANUQuest is a GPS-based orientation app that allows perople to discover a new place fast, in a gamified fashion, it does this by using quests and custom tours. While playing the game the user can gain discounts from the businesses around that location. Be it an unversity Campus, a part of a city or amid-large sized outdoor facility, this application allows all your visitors to quickly find out about the important places that they need to know. Currenlty we are developing this as an app aiding new students to find all the important places they could use around the campus. The game also gives them access to deals for the businesses operating in the area. The university is able to tailor the kiind of tasks and quests the students engage with and then analyse how the campus is being used in order to aid them with their decision making regarding the campus.
